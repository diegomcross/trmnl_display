Auto-Manager

Automatically tags weapons keep / favorite / junk by score and stages junk on a character to dismantle — but only while Destiny is running and you're in orbit, a social space, or matchmaking (never mid-activity). Exotics, locked, and equipped weapons are never touched. There's no dismantle API, so it moves junk onto a character for you to dismantle in-game.

Status

Off

Rules & thresholds

Watched weapons (you picked tracked perks) score by perk-match % — 100% = everything you tracked; copies under the watched-junk bar get junked. Unwatched weapons score by your ★ Perk-Finder favorites, grade-aware: each trait column counts its best favorited perk and 100% needs a 3★ favorite in BOTH columns (two 1★ favorites = 50%) — so ordinary rolls are never auto-favorited. A roll completing one of your saved Perk Finder combos never scores below the combo floor. A brand-new watched drop that beats your best kept copy is auto-kept with a different chime. Favorited copies are also locked. For weapons with multiple copies it keeps all favorites plus the single best-rated keep and junks the rest — never your last copy.
■ light green favorite = set by the app   ■ pink favorite = set by you (shown on the Weapon Vault / Watch / Drops tags).
