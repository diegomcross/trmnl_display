App Settings

Every knob of the vault tools in one place, in plain language. Changes save to the always-on server immediately — no restart needed.

Auto-Manager — on / off

Off

When ON, the app tags weapon copies keep / favorite / junk and stages junk on your character for dismantling — only while Destiny is running and you're in orbit or a social space, never mid-activity. Exotics, locked, and equipped weapons are never touched; it never junks your last copy. Full status and the decision log live on the Auto-Manager page.

Auto-Manager — score thresholds

Watched weapons (you picked tracked perks on Weapon Watch) score by how much of your wishlist the copy matches — 100% = everything you tracked.
Unwatched weapons score by your ★ Perk Finder favorites, grade-aware: each trait column counts its best favorited perk, and 100% needs a 3★ favorite in BOTH columns. Two 1★ favorites = 50%, so ordinary rolls are no longer auto-favorited — grade your favorites (1–3★) in Perk Finder to teach it what "great" means.
Combo-match floor: a roll that completes one of your saved Perk Finder combos never scores below this (default 80 = it gets kept), even if the individual perks aren't starred.

Auto-Manager — junk staging & safety

There is no dismantle API — the app moves junk-tagged items onto the chosen character (up to the per-slot number in each of Kinetic / Energy / Power — and, if armor staging is on, in each of Helmet / Gauntlets / Chest / Leg / Class Item) so you dismantle them in-game. It tops the piles back up as you dismantle. Armor is staging-only: the app never tags armor — it only moves pieces you already junked yourself. The two caps bound how much one pass can ever do, so a bad rule can't sweep your whole vault at once.

Auto-Manager — timing

In orbit/social the app checks at the first cadence (junk top-up speed). Inside an activity it only runs a cheap "did the activity end?" check at the second cadence — so tagging and junk staging kick in within seconds of finishing. While the game is closed the check is a no-op at the third cadence. Lower = snappier, higher = fewer Bungie API calls.

PVE / PVP roll tags

Loading your saved combos…

Every roll shows a PVP or PVE tag (Weapon Watch, Weapon Vault inspect, New Drops). A roll is PVP when it completes one of your saved combos whose role is pvp; anything else is PVE. Build and role-tag combos in Perk Finder.

Your favorites (drive unwatched scoring)

Loading favorites…

Grade favorites 1–3★ in Perk Finder. With everything at 1★ the app can't tell "nice" from "god-tier" — 2★ and 3★ are what push a roll into keep / favorite territory.

God-roll alerts (fixed rules)

A fresh drop of a watched weapon fires the PC chime + TRMNL panel alert and auto-locks when it matches at least 75% of your tracked wishlist with 3+ criteria hit out of 4+ tracked. These three numbers are deliberately fixed (they're the definition of "god roll"); ask Claude if you ever want them configurable.

Community data

Perk popularity comes from the DIM community wishlist; perk insights come from Clarity — the same community data DIM shows. Both refresh weekly on their own; force it here after a big patch.
